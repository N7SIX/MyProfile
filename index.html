About Me

Hello and 73! I'm N7SIX, a licensed Amateur Radio operator and open-source firmware hacker. My passion sits at the intersection of radio frequency (RF) experimentation and embedded software development — tearing apart cheap handheld transceivers to push them way beyond their factory limits.

I've been active in the amateur radio community, operating on HF, VHF, and UHF bands, and contributing to the growing ecosystem of open-source firmware for the popular Quansheng UV-K5 / UV-K6 / UV-5R Plus radio family.

When I'm not on the air, I'm reverse-engineering firmware, writing C code for Cortex-M microcontrollers, and collaborating with the global SDR and ham radio open-source community.
